Gabriel Abdo Alcântara
Filmmaker · Director of Photography · Photographer

Bio

Gabe is a filmmaker and Director of Photography based in Southern California. Born in São Paulo, Brazil, he grew up across Bogotá, Buenos Aires, and São Paulo before completing his education in Los Angeles.

He holds a BFA in Cinematography from Chapman University, with a minor in Design & Fabrication. Currently, Gabriel is co-directing and serving as Director of Photography on Carnival Cosmology — a documentary about the San Francisco Cacophony Society, produced alongside Mia since November 2023.
