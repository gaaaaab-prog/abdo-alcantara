BTS photography for an independent short film, coming soon.
All images shot on Mamiya 645 in Los Angeles, CA.

Gabe is a young filmmaker and photographer from São Paulo, Brazil. He just recently graduated from Chapman University with a BFA in Cinematography (Film Production), and a minor in Design & Fabrication. He spent his early childhood in Brazil, but started middle school in Bogotá (CO), part of high school in Buenos Aires (AR), and graduated back in São Paulo. Gabriel has been based in Southern California since 2019, where he completed his higher education and continues gaining experience in the various fields of technology, photography, art, and construction that never cease to amaze him. Currently, Gabriel is the Director of Photography, and Co-Director of a documentary about The San Francisco Cacophony Society. Carnival Cosmology recounts the jaw dropping feats and permanent imprint that this mislabeled counterculture group has left on San Francisco and beyond. Gabriel and Mia have been producing this project together since November 2023, and are in strides with post-production. He continues pursuing his passions in film, lighting design, and audio engineering.
